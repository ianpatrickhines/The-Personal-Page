Ian Patrick Hines is the founder & principal of Blarnify, a small digital communications & public relations studio. From 2010–2012, he served as a spokesman for the O'Malley Administration.

In his free time, he writes at length on his website, remarks on App.net, shares photos of his children on Instagram and listens to a lot of music.

He is a fourth-generation Marylander from Glen Burnie.
